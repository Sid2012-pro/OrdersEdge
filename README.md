WHAT IS ORDER'S EDGE?
This is a star wars fan game. It is a single player combat platformer based in the clone wars.
There are 8 characters available including -
JEDI
-Master Obi-Wan-Kenobi or OBI-WAN as referred to in game
-Ahsoka Tano or simply AHSOKA as referred to in game
-Anakin Skywalker or simply ANAKIN as referred to in game
-Master Mace Windu or simply MACE as referred to in game
-Master Kit Fisto or simply KIT as referred to in game
-Master Plo Koon or simply PLO as referred to in game
Clone Commanders
-Commander Rex or simply REX as referred to in game
-Commander Cody or simply CODY as referred to in game
